Title: Retrieval + planning notes

Key ideas:
- Use topical keywords derived from the topic and user interests.
- Score documents with a lightweight heuristic (TF-IDF style).
- Prefer diverse source types: reports, surveys, blogs, news.

Planning approach:
- Scope statement: define boundaries and exclusions.
- Questions: foundational definitions, current landscape, disagreements.
- Gaps: explicitly list missing evidence or viewpoints.

Signals that the plan is not ready:
- Less than 3 sources or sources are all the same type.
- No contradictions or disagreements identified.
- Missing evaluation or benchmark discussions.
