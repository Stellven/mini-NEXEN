Title: Evaluation metrics cheat sheet

Possible metrics for agentic research systems:
- Coverage: fraction of relevant subtopics represented.
- Depth: number of layers in the outline with evidence.
- Diversity: number of source types and perspectives.
- Traceability: every claim links to a source brief.
- Timeliness: recentness of sources for fast-moving topics.

Risks:
- Overfitting to interests and missing external viewpoints.
- Skipping contradictory sources.
- Treating plan as final narrative instead of a roadmap.
